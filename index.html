Codeforces 1172C2 Nauuo and Pictures (hard version)

發表於 2019-10-16 | 更新於 2019-10-18 | 分類於 Codeforces | 評論數：

Nauuo is a girl who loves random picture website, there are $N$ pictures and she visits the website $M$ times.
The website doesnt display each picture with equal probability, the probability of $i-th$ picture is $\frac{w_i}{\sum w_i}$
However, Nauuo wants the picture she likes displays more often, so when she saw a picture she likes, she would add $1$ to its weight;
otherwise, she would substract $1$.

  • $1\leq n\leq 2\cdot 10^5$
  • $1\leq m\leq 3000$
  • $\sum w_i\leq 998244352 - m$

不斷重複以上操作，題目要求從某點開始 Travel 的路上有哪些點經過無限次，輸出經過無限次的點的總數

  • $1\leq N,M \leq 2\times 10^5$
  • $0\leq x_i,t_i,p_j,b_j \leq 10^9$
